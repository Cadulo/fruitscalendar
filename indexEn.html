Advantages of consuming seasonal fruit

Choosing seasonal fruits and vegetables is an investment in a more sustainable, cost-effective, and healthy world. They have better flavor, color, aroma, and nutritional contribution because they are consumed at an optimal time. Furthermore, because of the high supply, we can get better prices while supporting the local economy and reducing waste.
